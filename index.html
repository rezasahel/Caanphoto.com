About Us

Caan Photo is the first online marketplace for buying and selling photos, videos, paintings, and graphics in Afghanistan, established in late 2018. The purpose of launching this website is to create a creative, educational, and commercial environment for artists to directly showcase and sell their works. The founders of this platform is Reza Sahel (photographer). One of the significant challenges faced by photographers, designers, and artists in Afghanistan and beyond is the difficulty in selling their creations both domestically and internationally. While numerous websites operate in this field globally, Afghan artists, due to various limitations, including Afghan banking regulations and security issues, often cannot directly offer their work to customers.
